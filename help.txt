[Help]
To show all functions available in this chatbot.

[Case]
1. Hong Kong COVID-19 Information

a. Latest Statistics
To know the latest COVID-19 Statistics in Hong Kong which update everyday.
		
b. List of Buildings
To list all buildings with confirmed cases of COVID-19 in the past 14 days.
		
c. Quarantine Centres
To show all Quarantine Centres set up by HK Government in Hong Kong.
	
	
2. Worldwide COVID-19 Dashboard

a. HK CHP
To show the dashboard provided by HK Government.
			
b. China CDC
To show the dashboard provided by Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention.
		
c. WHO
To show the dashboard provided by World Health Organization.


3. Additional Function
You can type "Case XXXX" to know the latest COVID-19 Statistics in Hong Kong which update everyday.
For example: Case 1001

[Clinic]
1. All General Out-Patient Clinics

a. Hong Kong Island
To display the location, contact and opening hours of all General Out-Patient Clinics in Hong Kong Island.
	
b. Kowloon
To display the location, contact and opening hours of all General Out-Patient Clinics in Kowloon.
	
c. New Territories
To display the location, contact and opening hours of all General Out-Patient Clinics in New Territories.


2. Special Service Arrangement
	
a. Non-office Hours
To show the opening hours of evening, sundays & public holidays' General Out-Patient Clinics in 2020.
	
b. Re-arrangement
To show the service arrangement under the situation of Severe Respiratory Disease associated with a Novel Infectious Agent.
	
c. Extreme Weather
To show the special arrangement of General Out-Patient Clinics for Tropical Cyclone Warning / "Extreme Condition" and Rainstorm Warning.
